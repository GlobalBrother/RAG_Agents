1. **Concept 1 – “The MS Patient Who Built Her Own Apothecary”**

   Why: Direct MS story, “been there” credibility, hope + practical remedies for flares, pain, fatigue, brain fog.

   Strongest: still **Audience 1**, because “rheumatologist / diagnosis / flares” aligns perfectly.

3. **Concept 3 – “The ‘Flare Day’ Drawer”**  
   Best match: **Audience 1 – Autoimmune**  
   Why: “Flare day” is autoimmune language; they want concrete tools on bad days (pain, fatigue, brain fog).

   Best match: **Audience 7 – Chronic joint pain & stiffness cutting back on NSAIDs**  
   Why: Speaks directly to pain + NSAID concerns, shows plant-based alternatives.

5. **Concept 5 – “The Autoimmune Panic Basket”**  
   Best match: **Audience 1 – Autoimmune**  
   Why: “What if this gets worse?” is their core fear; basket of remedies = control instead of dread.

6. **Concept 6 – “Big Pharma Doesn’t Make This… So I Did”**  
   Best matches:  
   - **Audience 1 – Autoimmune** (feel dismissed, want more than meds)

   Strongest: **Audience 2**, because anti‑Big‑Pharma + self-sufficiency is their daily mindset.

   Best matches:

9. **Concept 9 – “The 3 A.M. Flare Search Bar”**

    Best matches:
